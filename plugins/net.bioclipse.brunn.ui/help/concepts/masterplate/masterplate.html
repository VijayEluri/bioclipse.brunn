Master Plate

A master plate defines which compound marker correspond to which compound and with what concentration. So a master plate corresponds to how substances are placed on a batch of plates.

Creating a Master Plate

To create a master plate, right click on the folder named "Master Plates" or any of it's subfolders and choose "Create Master Plate", type a name in the dialog box and choose a plate layout for the new master plate. You will also be asked to type in the number of plates in the batch so the system can keep track of how many plates are left in cryo store

Editing a Master Plate

By double-clicking on a master plate, you can open it in an editor where you can connect well markers with substances by dragging and dropping and create dilution series.

Creating a Dilution Serie

Dilution series are created through connecting a substance to a marker by dragging the substance from the Brunn Explorer and into the list of markers at the bottom part of the editor. A dialog pops up and asks for information on how to perform dilution over wells with that marker. It is possible to do the dilution horisontally or vertically and wells with the marker in the other direction will become replicates.
